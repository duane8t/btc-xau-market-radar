Setup & Q&A

How to use BTC XAU Market Radar correctly and safely.

Step 1 – Join Telegram

BTC XAU Market Radar operates entirely through Telegram.

  • Tap Join Live Market Radar on the main page
  • Ensure notifications are enabled for Telegram
  • Pin the channel for easy access

Step 2 – Understand What Alerts Mean

Alerts indicate market activity, not trade instructions.

  • Liquidity sweeps
  • Volatility expansion
  • Strong directional momentum
  • Market reaction after key levels

Alerts are informational only. They do not tell you to buy or sell.

Step 3 – When Alerts Will NOT Appear

  • Low volatility conditions
  • Choppy or range-bound markets
  • Weekends or reduced session liquidity
  • After major moves when price is consolidating

No alert means conditions are not favourable. This is intentional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a signal service?

No. BTC XAU Market Radar does not provide buy or sell signals.

Does it auto-trade?

No. There is no automated trading, execution, or account access.

How often will alerts appear?

Only when conditions are valid. Some days may have multiple alerts, others may have none.

Why hasn’t it alerted yet?

This usually means market conditions are not suitable. Silence is a filter, not a failure.

Can I trade directly from alerts?

Trading decisions are entirely your responsibility. Always confirm with your own analysis and risk management.

Is this financial advice?

No. This service provides market awareness only.

Which markets are monitored?

Bitcoin (BTCUSD) is monitored 24/7. Gold (XAUUSD) is monitored during London and New York sessions.
