BTC XAU Market Radar is a real-time market monitoring service designed to identify when Bitcoin (BTC) and Gold (XAU) become active.

It focuses on liquidity events, volatility expansion, and market reaction — not predictions.

What It Does

  • Monitors BTCUSD 24/7
  • Monitors XAUUSD during London & New York sessions
  • Detects liquidity sweeps and volatility expansion
  • Confirms market reaction before bias is suggested
  • Issues stand-down alerts during poor conditions

What It Is Not

  • No buy or sell signals
  • No automated trading
  • No performance claims
  • No over-alerting or hype
